Frequently Asked Questions

How do you catch snakes?

We use specialised tools and proven techniques to safely capture and relocate snakes. The process is designed to minimise stress on the snake while ensuring the safety of everyone on-site. Whether the snake is indoors or outdoors, we handle each situation with care and efficiency.

Are snakes dangerous to pets?

Yes, some snakes can be dangerous to pets, especially venomous species. Even non-venomous snakes may bite if they feel threatened. It’s important to keep pets away from any snake and seek professional help for safe removal.

What areas do you service?

We provide snake removal services in Heyfield, Tinamba, Coongulla, Glenmaggie, Seaton, Cowwarr and surrounding areas, including both residential and rural properties. If you’re unsure whether we cover your location, feel free to reach out for confirmation.

How much do you charge?

The fees depend on the type of service required and the location. We strive to offer fair and competitive pricing. Contact us for a customised quote, and we’ll provide all the details upfront.

Can you remove venomous snakes?

Yes, we're fully trained and equipped to handle venomous snakes safely. The priority is always to remove the snake without harm to anyone, providing peace of mind in potentially dangerous situations.
